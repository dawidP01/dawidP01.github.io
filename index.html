History of Strength Centre

In 1982 our previous owner James Siby, founded Strength Centre in a small location right beside Salthill beach. When we opened we were only one of 3 gyms in the entire city. We opened with only a small gym that offered no cardio equipment whatsoever, just a couple of racks, weights and benches. Even though we were a very small company we managed to grow fast due to our expert staff, friendly atmosphere and because of our amazing location.

Now it's almost 40 years since we opened, we have expanded into one of the biggest health centres in the county. In 2019 our new owner Elizabeth, inherited our complex and has run it ever since. We have plans in the upcoming years to open new branches in Cork and Naas.

Location

We are Located at 26 Woodquay Street , which is a prime location right in the centre of town, right beside the Salmon Weir Bridge. Strength Centre is just 5 minutes away from Eyre Square. Our location easily reached by foot, but if you are coming from further away there is an option to take the bus. Franciscan Friarya bus stop is just around the corner from us.
